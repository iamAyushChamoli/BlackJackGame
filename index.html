Rules:

  • The Player begins by clicking on "Start!" Button and three cards at random are drawn
  • The sum of the values of the cards is observed and the game progresses based on on of the following three outputs:
    • The Sum > 21 in which case the player loses and has to Restart the game
    • The Sum < 21 in which case the player is allowed to draw another card whose value is also added to the sum
    • The Sum = 21 in which case the Player Wins
  • The Face Cards are of value 10.
  • This continues until the player either Wins or Loses
